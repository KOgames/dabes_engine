Module body

A Body is the representation of an Entity in physics space.

As such, it is only relevant in ??? driven by physics engines.

The Body is used to represent the Entity in the physical space. The ??? represents it in the graphical space. The Body dimensions, rotation, and position determine how the Sprite is drawn.

If you need to set a collision area that is smaller than the Sprite's cell size, use set_hit_box.

Body extends BoundObject
